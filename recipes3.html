Pulao

Veg Pulav is also called as Vegetable Pulao, Pilaf is an assortment of veggies in fluffy rice & garnished with nuts & herbs.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Vegetables
  • Spices & Herbs
  • Nuts

Steps

  1. Cooking rice and veggies perfectly is the key to great tasting pulao.
  2. Heat 2 tablespoon + 1 teaspoon oil in the same pan add cashews fry until golden. Remove and transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  3. Now add 1/2 inch cinnamon, 2 nos cloves, 1 small bayleaf, 1 small star anise, a small piece mace and 1/2 teaspoon shahi jeera. Let it splutter. You can replace it with cumin seeds if you do not have shahi jeera.
  4. Add 1 long green chilli, onion along with salt.
  5. Saute until its golden.
  6. Add cooked veggies and saute for a minute.
  7. Now add 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder, 1/4 teaspoon pepper powder and give a quick mix.
  8. Now add cooked rice and mix it.
